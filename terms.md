# Terms and Conditions — Missed Call Gateway

**Program Name:** Missed Call Gateway
**Description:** Automated SMS responses to callers who reach a business when the line is unavailable, to help schedule appointments.
**Message Frequency:** Up to 10 messages per conversation.
**Message and Data Rates:** Standard message and data rates may apply.
**Support:** Reply HELP for help, or contact shraiber4@gmail.com.
**Opt-Out:** Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages.

**HELP:** Reply HELP for assistance.

**STOP:** Reply STOP to opt out. You will receive one confirmation message and no further SMS.
