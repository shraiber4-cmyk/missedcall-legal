# Privacy Policy — Missed Call Gateway

Last updated: April 22, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect your phone number when you place an inbound call to a business using our service. We also collect SMS conversation content that you provide in response to our messages.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
Your phone number and SMS messages are used solely to respond to your service inquiry and help the business you contacted schedule an appointment.

## 3. Information Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number, message content, or any personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. Data Retention
Conversation data is retained for operational and support purposes and deleted upon request.

## 5. Opt-Out
You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.
